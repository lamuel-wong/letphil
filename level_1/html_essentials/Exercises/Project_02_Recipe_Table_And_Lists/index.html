Cooking Steps

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Pan-fry the chicken until golden brown.
  3. Heat Teriyaki sauce in a saucepan then coat the chicken in the sauce
  4. Plate the meal and store leftovers for later.

Ingredients Table

Ingredient Amount Notes

Rice 1 cup Steam in a rice cooker
Teriyaki Sauce 4 tablespoons Use for marinade
Ground Beef 2 lbs Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika
Spinach 1 bunch Wash thoroughly before adding to the pan
